Our Speciality Packages

Take a look at some of our rooms and speciality packages! We have several special occasions packages, especially or newlyweds and couples celebrating an anniversary.


Our four bedroom’s names are Sweetheart Haven, Treetop, Enchantment, and Out of Africa. Select the “Check Availability” button for special deals, packages, and room pricing.
Reservations must be secured by deposit or credit card equal to one night’s lodging. Cash, personal check, Traveler’s check, Master Card, Visa and Discover are accepted. Children by special arrangement, Sorry no pets. Smoking is permitted on outside porches only.

These rooms have one or two queen-size beds, a full private bath, and air conditioning. Fireplaces and/or private porches in some of the rooms of this Ohio Amish Country inn help create the perfect setting for a romantic vacation. Many rooms also feature trundle beds.
